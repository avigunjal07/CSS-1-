Pune : A City of Heritage, Culture, and Education


It holds a significant place in Indian history as the seat of the Peshwas during the Maratha Empire

Pune is considered the cultural capital of Maharashtra.

 Pune stands as a premier educational hub, hosting renowned institutions such as Savitribai Phule Pune University, Fergusson College, COEP, IISER Pune, and Symbiosis International University. These institutions attract students from all over India and the world, making Pune a youthful, dynamic, and intellectually stimulating city that harmoniously blends tradition with modernity.
